# Platform Roadmap

Focusing on the long-term intelligence layer and the expansion of Atlas into a comprehensive mobility governance platform.

## Access & Equity
- [ ] **Isochrone Modeling**: Real-time walking/cycling access maps generated from the GTFS stop-graph.
- [ ] **Equity Overlays**: Demographic and socio-economic census integration to measure "Access to Opportunity" across different neighborhood clusters.
- [ ] **Coverage Scoring**: Automated scoring of how well high-frequency routes serve priority equity zones.

## Network Optimization (Generative)
- [ ] **Macro-Optimizer**: AI-driven network design that proposes route realignments based on population demand patterns and transit-oriented development.
- [ ] **Intervention Simulator**: Multivariate testing—combining frequency bumps with TSP (Transit Signal Priority) and signal-timing improvements.

## Ecosystem & Collaboration
- [ ] **Atlas Share**: Public engagement portals where scenarios (e.g., "The Division BRT Proposal") can be shared via URL for community feedback.
- [ ] **Multi-Agency Federation**: Capability to merge feeds from adjacent regional agencies into a single "Super-Network" view.

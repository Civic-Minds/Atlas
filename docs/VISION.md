# Product Vision

Atlas aims to eliminate the "technical friction" of transit planning by automating network auditing, impact simulation, and strategic optimization through high-fidelity transit intelligence and AI orchestration.

## The North Star

- **Continuous Network Auditor**: An autonomous background engine that identifies service reliability gaps and frequency claim discrepancies without manual data scrubbing.
- **Predictive Mobility Trajectory**: High-fidelity modeling of travel-time savings and ridership impact across 5–10 year capital investment cycles.
- **Generative Network Synthesis**: AI-driven route realignment and optimization that balances population density, job access, and equity targets.

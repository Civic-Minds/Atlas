# Roadmap

Atlas is a transit intelligence platform that transforms raw vehicle positions into operational insights. This roadmap outlines the path from a prototype ingestion engine to a comprehensive mobility platform.

- **[Vision](docs/VISION.md)**: Product vision statement and long-term North Star goals.
- **[Product](docs/ROADMAP_PRODUCT.md)**: UX, performance visualization, mapping features, and reporting tools.
- **[Technical](docs/ROADMAP_TECHNICAL.md)**: Service layer modularization, database scaling (TimescaleDB), and deployment hardening.
- **[Platform](docs/ROADMAP_PLATFORM.md)**: Multi-agency expansion, cross-regional benchmarks, and commercial APIs.

Behind the scenes, we are building **[NextGen Atlas](docs/ROADMAP_NEXTGEN.md)**: a high-precision modeling engine focusing on automated OTP (On-Time Performance) aggregation, spatial fallback matching, and ghost-bus identification.
